About

Long Shadow Love started as a weekend experiment exploring the possible use of long shadows on iOS 7.

With all the traction gained on Behance, I received tonnes of requests to make this into an icon theme for Winterboard; and so I present to you my very first Cydia theme. I hope you like it.

A big shoutout to redittors Koby Jordan and Kevin Durham for helping me develop and beta test.

Who am I?

I am a student of Human Computer Interaction at Georgia Tech. When I am not drooling over long shadows and blurred backgrounds, I like to play badminton.
